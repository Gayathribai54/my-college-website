Government Hussainialam college

Government Degree and PG College for Women, Hussaini Alam, was established in 1984 to provide higher education opportunities for women in Hyderabad's old city. The institution is an affiliated college operating under the jurisdiction of Osmania University, Hyderabad, which means it follows the curriculum and academic calendar provided by the university. It has been accredited by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC), previously holding a 'B' grade. The college offers a diverse range of undergraduate (UG) and postgraduate (PG) courses across Arts, Commerce, and Science streams. Specific programs include B.A. (e.g., History, Political Science, Public Administration), B.Com. (General and Computer Applications), and B.Sc. (e.g., Microbiology, Zoology, Chemistry, Botany, Computer Science). PG courses include M.A. Urdu, M.Com, and M.Sc Microbiology.
